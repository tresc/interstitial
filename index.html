The Interstitial Studies Archive is an ongoing academic project maintained by the Department of Anomalous Studies at Dunmore University. It documents the history, mechanics, and human consequences of the card game known as Interstitial, the force known as the Substrate, and the organisation known as the Pale Commission.

The Archive does not take positions on whether the phenomena described herein are real in the strictest sense. It takes the position that they are documented, and that documentation is what the Archive does.
