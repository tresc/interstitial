— and I understand that this is not a complaint. I am not filing a complaint. I want to be clear that I understand the distinction between a complaint and a documentation. The reflection situation has stabilised at 0.9 seconds, which I am told is on the higher end of Stage Three normal. I don't find it distressing. I find it informative. The reflection always catches up. I've started thinking of it as a lag in correspondence — the message takes a little longer to arrive than it used to. I am still receiving the message. The message is correct. My skin. I want to write about my skin because I don't think anyone writes about this part honestly. The texture is not unpleasant. That's the honest thing I want to say. I expected it to be unpleasant and it is not. It is cool and regular and there is a geometry to it that I find, when I trace it, coherent. Not in a way I can explain. In a way I can only trace. Like the notation on the slabs — I have seen photographs of the slabs in the Ostroff monograph and yes, it is like that, and I want that on record because I think it matters and I don't think people say it plainly: the Substrate is writing on us and it is not ugly. It is extremely precise. I slept last on — I don't remember. It's been some time. I don't miss it. I sit at night in a way that seems to function similarly.

I don't know anyone named Clara. I'm leaving it. [fragment ends]
