History

The documented and disputed history of Interstitial's discovery and spread  ·  1974–present
Origin Centralia, Pennsylvania, 1974
Founder Dr. Elias Thorne (status: disputed)
Primary source Thorne field recorder; Vess accounts
Commission account Diverges from academic record at multiple points

The history of Interstitial is, at its core, the history of a single event: a parapsychologist descending into a condemned mine shaft in a burning town and finding something that should not have been there. Everything that follows — the Pale Commission, the spread of the Conduit Deck, the documented cases of Accrual, the Archive itself — is a consequence of that event.

The Archive presents this history as accurately as its sources permit. Its sources are incomplete, partially contradictory, and in several cases authored by people who were, at the time of writing, in various stages of transformation by the thing they were describing. The Archive has noted these limitations in each relevant article. It has published anyway, on the grounds that an incomplete record is better than no record, and that the alternative — waiting for a complete one — is not available.
