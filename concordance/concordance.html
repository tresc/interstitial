The Concordance is an ongoing project to document individual cards across known decks. Each entry covers depiction, mechanical function, Accrual cost, known appearances, recovered notes, and unresolved questions. The Archive does not claim this list is complete. The Archive does not claim any list of this deck's contents could be complete. It is documenting what it has found.

Researchers attempting to produce a complete enumeration of Conduit Deck cards are advised to stop counting. The count is not the point.
