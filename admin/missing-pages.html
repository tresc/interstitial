This page documents pages that should exist in the Archive but do not, for various reasons. The Archive maintains it as a matter of institutional honesty: it is easier to know what you have if you also know what you are missing. The Archive notes that this logic also applies to the documents it studies, and that the Substrate's record may operate on a similar principle — that knowing what is absent is part of knowing what is present. The Archive did not intend this observation to be unsettling. It has noted that it is.
