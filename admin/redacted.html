The Archive's policy is to publish what it documents, with appropriate notation. Redaction is the exception. When content is withheld, the Archive publishes the fact of the withholding here, with whatever explanation is available. The Archive believes that a redaction notice is more honest than a gap that readers might not notice. It also believes that readers deserve to know when they are not being told everything.

The Archive is not always being told everything. This page is part of how it tells you that.
